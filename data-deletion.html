Data Deletion — HowlBox AV Media Collector

App: HowlBox AV Media Collector  ·  Developer: Wolfstrata

Effective date: June 19, 2026

This page explains how to delete the data associated with HowlBox AV Media Collector (the “App”), what is deleted, and what (if anything) is kept.

Where your data lives

Your collection — the items you scan or add, their details, condition, values, and any images cached for them — is stored only on your device. The App does not require an account, and we do not keep a server-side copy of your collection.

How to delete your data

  1. Delete within the App. Open the App and remove individual items, or use the Backup/Manage options to clear your collection.
  2. Uninstall the App (deletes everything on the device). Uninstalling HowlBox AV Media Collector immediately and permanently removes all of its on-device data, including your collection database, settings, and any cached images. The App is configured so this data is not backed up to the cloud or transferred to another device, so it does not survive an uninstall.
  3. Request deletion of any server-side data. If you would like us to delete any data that may be associated with your use of our backend service, email rperumal@wolfstrata.com with the subject line Data deletion request. We will action verified requests within 30 days.

What is deleted

  • Your entire on-device collection, settings, and cached images (on uninstall, or when you clear data in the App).
  • Any logs or temporary data associated with your requests on our backend, upon a verified deletion request.

What is kept, and for how long

  • Images you scan: transmitted to our backend and AI processor only to return an identification/valuation result, and not retained beyond what is needed to service the request.
  • Valuation results: may be cached briefly (typically up to 24 hours) to improve performance and reduce cost; this cache is not tied to your identity.
  • Backend request logs: standard operational logs (e.g. timestamps, IP address for rate limiting/abuse prevention) are retained only as long as needed to operate the service and are purged for your requests upon a verified deletion request.
  • Purchases: subscription/transaction records are handled by Google Play under Google’s policies and are not controlled by this App.
  • Files you exported yourself: if you used the App’s manual Backup feature to save a file to your own storage or cloud, that file is under your control; delete it from wherever you saved it.
