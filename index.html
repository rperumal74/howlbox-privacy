Privacy Policy — HowlBox AV Media Collector

Effective date: June 19, 2026

HowlBox AV Media Collector (the “App”) is operated by Wolfstrata (“we”, “us”). This policy explains what information the App handles, why, and your choices. By using the App you agree to this policy.

1. Summary

  • The App helps you catalog physical audio/video media (vinyl, CDs, cassettes, and similar) and estimate their value.
  • Your collection is stored locally on your device.
  • When you choose to scan an item or use an AI feature, the relevant photo and item details are sent to our backend server and to our AI processor (Anthropic) to identify the item and estimate its value.
  • We do not sell your data and we do not show ads.

2. Information we handle

Photos and images you capture or select. When you scan a cover, sleeve, label, or barcode, the image is processed on your device (text and barcode recognition) and, for AI identification/valuation, transmitted over an encrypted (HTTPS) connection to our backend and to Anthropic for processing.

Item and collection details. Information you enter or that is recognized about your media (title, artist, format, condition, notes, estimated and custom values). This is stored locally; relevant fields may be sent with a scan to improve identification and valuation.

Purchase status. If you subscribe, Google Play processes the payment. We receive a subscription/entitlement status from Google Play Billing; we do not receive or store your full payment card details.

Technical data. Our backend may log standard request metadata (such as IP address and timestamps) to operate the service, prevent abuse, and apply rate limits. We do not use this to build advertising profiles.

The App does not require you to create an account and does not collect your name, email, contacts, precise location, or device identifiers for advertising.

3. How we use information

  • To identify scanned media and estimate its value (core functionality).
  • To operate, secure, and rate-limit our backend service.
  • To provide subscription (Premium) features via Google Play.

4. Sharing and third parties

We share data only as needed to provide the App:

  • Anthropic, PBC — processes scan images and item details to generate identification and valuation results, under its applicable terms.
  • Hosting provider — our backend is hosted on Render; data passes through their infrastructure to deliver responses.
  • Google Play — processes subscriptions and delivers app updates.
  • Optional reference data sources (e.g. pricing/metadata providers) may receive a title/identifier to look up public information, where enabled.

We do not sell personal information.

5. Data retention

Your collection stays on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App. Images are transmitted for processing and are not retained by us for longer than needed to return a result; short-lived caching of valuation results (typically up to 24 hours) may occur to improve performance and reduce cost. Backend request logs are retained only as long as needed for operation and abuse prevention.

6. Your choices and rights

  • You can use the App to catalog items manually without scanning, which avoids sending images off-device.
  • You can export or delete your local data from within the App.
  • You can uninstall the App to remove all locally stored data from your device.
  • To request deletion of any data associated with you on our backend, contact us using the details below.

7. Security

Data in transit is protected with HTTPS/TLS. Optional encrypted backups use industry-standard encryption with a password you choose. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

8. Children

The App is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Disclaimer of value estimates

Value estimates and condition grades are informational only, generated in part by automated/AI methods, and are not guarantees of authenticity, condition, or resale price. HowlBox is not affiliated with or endorsed by any label, artist, distributor, or grading authority.

10. Changes

We may update this policy; we will revise the “Effective date” above and, where appropriate, notify you in the App.
